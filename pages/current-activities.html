Activities in 2025-2026

Conference organization

  • Prof. Tayo Obafemi-Ajayi serves as Technical Program Chair for the IEEE Conference on Computational Intelligence in Bioinformatics and Computational Biology (IEEE CIBCB 2025) and the IEEE-EMBS International Conference on Biomedical and Health Informatics (IEEE EMBS BHI 2025).
  • Dr. Leo Zhang serves as the Local Chair for the 33rd IEEE/ACM International Symposium on Quality of Service (IWQoS 2025) and the 28th International Symposium on Research in Attacks, Intrusions and Defenses (RAID 2025).

Special sessions, workshops/symposiums, and competitions

  • Dr. Catherine Huang and Prof. Tayo Obafemi-Ajayi organized and participated in special sessions on Ethical AI at IJCNN 2025, and served as a panelist on Responsible AI for CAI 2025.
  • Dr. Catherine Huang will serve as the IJCNN 2026 data competition chair.

Tutorials, keynote and plenary talks, and webinars

  • Prof. Huiyu Zhou gave invited talks and keynotes at ICIPAI 2025, AIPR 2025, ODIN'25 with MICCAI, and CVIPPR 2025.

Journal special issues

  • Dr. Peilan Xu, Prof. Wenjian Luo, and Dr. Hansheng Lei launched an Applied Soft Computing special issue on Robust and Secure AI Systems, inviting cutting-edge research on adversarial robustness and security.
  • Professor Wenjian Luo, Professor Maoguo Gong, and Professor Ishfaq Ahmad launched a Journal of Information Security and Applications special issue on Security and Privacy in Large AI Models.

Other events aiming to promote the research, application and education relevant to Neural Networks

  • Dr. Catherine Huang serves as the NNTC vice-chair on communications and education for 2025-2026.
