## Activities in 2025-2026

### Conference organization

- Prof. Tayo Obafemi-Ajayi serves as Technical Program Chair for the IEEE Conference on Computational Intelligence in Bioinformatics and Computational Biology ([IEEE CIBCB 2025](https://smile.ee.ncku.edu.tw/cibcb2025/)) and the IEEE-EMBS International Conference on Biomedical and Health Informatics ([IEEE EMBS BHI 2025](https://bhi.embs.org/2025/)).
- Dr. Leo Zhang serves as the Local Chair for the 33rd IEEE/ACM International Symposium on Quality of Service ([IWQoS 2025](https://iwqos2025.ieee-iwqos.org/)) and the 28th International Symposium on Research in Attacks, Intrusions and Defenses ([RAID 2025](https://raid2025.github.io/)).

### Special sessions, workshops/symposiums, and competitions

- Dr. Catherine Huang and Prof. Tayo Obafemi-Ajayi organized and participated in special sessions on [Ethical AI](https://2025.ijcnn.org/authors/special-sessions) at [IJCNN 2025](https://2025.ijcnn.org/), and served as a panelist on Responsible AI for [CAI 2025](https://cai.ieee.org/2025/).
- Dr. Catherine Huang will serve as the IJCNN 2026 data competition chair.

### Tutorials, keynote and plenary talks, and webinars

- Prof. Huiyu Zhou gave invited talks and keynotes at [ICIPAI 2025](https://www.icipai.org/), [AIPR 2025](https://aipr.net/index.html), [ODIN'25](https://odin-workshops.org/2025/index.html) with MICCAI, and [CVIPPR 2025](https://www.cvippr.net/index.html).

### Journal special issues

- Dr. Peilan Xu, Prof. Wenjian Luo, and Dr. Hansheng Lei launched an [Applied Soft Computing](https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/applied-soft-computing) special issue on [Robust and Secure AI Systems](https://www.sciencedirect.com/special-issue/319241/robust-and-secure-ai-systems), inviting cutting-edge research on adversarial robustness and security.
- Professor Wenjian Luo, Professor Maoguo Gong, and Professor Ishfaq Ahmad launched a [Journal of Information Security and Applications](https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/journal-of-information-security-and-applications) special issue on [Security and Privacy in Large AI Models](https://www.sciencedirect.com/special-issue/10WH8F246FZ).

### Other events aiming to promote the research, application and education relevant to Neural Networks

- Dr. Catherine Huang serves as the NNTC vice-chair on communications and education for 2025-2026.
