## 2022-2023

### Conference Organization

- C. Huang served as Technical Co-chair for [IEEE ICDIS 2022](https://www.icdis.org/).
- C. Huang served as Publicity Co-chair for [IEEE SSCI 2022](https://ieeessci2022.org/).

### Special Sessions, Workshops/Symposiums, and Competitions

- S. See and C. Huang participated in the WCCI 2022 Industry Day Panel on ML application for Security and Privacy.

### Tutorials, Keynote and Plenary Talks, and Webinars

- Huiyu Zhou was invited to talk at [CMI'22](https://www.confjuly.org/conference/CMI2022/), [FAIML'22](https://www.faiml.org/sep2022), [ECNLPIR'22](https://www.ecnlpir.org/kn2022), [ICBIBE'22](http://www.icbibe.org/KS.aspx), AICS'22, [ICIVIS'22](http://www.icivis.net/workshop/), [ICHVR'22](https://www.ichvr.org/), [CSAE'22](http://www.csaeconf.org/Default.aspx), [ICIS'22](http://www.intsci.ac.cn/icis2022/ksi/), [MLIS'22](http://www.machinelearningconf.org/Speaker?keynote=true), and CFAIS'22.
- Dipankar Dasgupta offered the following talks:
  - Topic 1: [Computational Intelligence in Cybersecurity](https://cis.ieee.org/images/files/Documents/DLP/DLP-charter/Dipankar_Dasgupta_Topic_1.pdf).
  - Topic 2: [Adversarial Machine Learning and Defense Strategies](https://cis.ieee.org/images/files/Documents/DLP/DLP-charter/Dipankar_Dasgupta_Topic_2.pdf).
  - Topic 3: [Adaptive Multi-Factor Authentication and Cyber Identity](https://cis.ieee.org/images/files/Documents/DLP/DLP-charter/Dipankar_Dasgupta_Topic_3.pdf).
  - Topic 4: [Advances in Immunological Computation](https://cis.ieee.org/images/files/Documents/DLP/DLP-charter/Dipankar_Dasgupta_Topic_4.pdf).
  - Topic 5: [AI vs AI: Viewpoints](https://cis.ieee.org/images/files/Documents/DLP/DLP-charter/Dipankar_Dasgupta_Topic_5.pdf).

### Journal Special Issues

- Zhou Tao, Chen Li, Lin Gu, Changming Sun, and Huiyu Zhou led the Frontiers in Radiology research topic "Computer-aided diagnosis based on medical image: trends and future research".
- W. Luo, Y. Jin, and C. Huang led the [IEEE Transactions on Artificial Intelligence special issue on Security and Privacy in Machine Learning](http://www.wikicfp.com/cfp/servlet/event.showcfp?eventid=139624&copyownerid=103669). The [submission guideline](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1axGRmh28yAUpJnkG6b7HTpVN2RXAKEhH/view?usp=sharing) is also archived.
- C. Huang, Y. Ong, and C. Fralick led the [Springer Complex & Intelligent Systems special issue on Secure Learning](https://www.springer.com/journal/40747/updates/18415824). The [submission guidelines](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1lYLQ1Abs8MdUIdRQEYq1qX4nWefB52TH/view?usp=sharing) are also archived.

## 2021-2022

### Conference Organization

- Dipankar Dasgupta organized Computational Intelligence in Cyber Security (CICS) at the IEEE Symposium Series on Computational Intelligence (SSCI), Singapore, December 4-7, 2022.

### Tutorials, Keynote and Plenary Talks, and Webinars

- Huiyu Zhou was invited to talk at [ICAIBD'22](http://icaibd.org/speakers.html), [ICIPMC'22](http://www.icipmc.net/keynote.html), [ICACDS'22](https://icacds.com/index.php#spks), Huazhong University of Science and Technology, NUAA, Guangxi Minzu University, and Dalian University of Technology.
- Dipankar Dasgupta delivered the tutorial "Machine Learning Applications, Adversarial Attacks and Mitigation Strategies" on December 5 at IEEE Symposium Series on Computational Intelligence (SSCI), December 4-7, 2022.

### Other Events Aiming to Promote the Research, Application and Education Relevant to Neural Networks

- Singh, Amit Kumar, Huiyu Zhou, and Stefano Berretti. "Guest Editorial: Medical Data Security Solution for Healthcare Industries." IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics 18, no. 8 (2022): 5558-5560.
- Dasgupta, Dipankar, and Kishor Datta Gupta. "Dual-filtering (DF) schemes for learning systems to prevent adversarial attacks." Complex & Intelligent Systems (2022): 1-22.
- Dipankar Dasgupta and Sajib Sen. "An Empirical Study of Algorithmic Bias." Handbook on Computer Learning and Intelligence, Vol. II, pages 895-922, World Scientific Publisher, 2022.
- Arunava Roy and Dipankar Dasgupta. "A Robust Framework for Adaptive Selection of Filter Ensembles to Detect Adversarial Inputs." Proceedings of the 5th International Workshop on Dependable and Secure Machine Learning, co-located with the [52nd IEEE/IFIP International Conference on Dependable Systems and Networks (DSN 2022)](https://dsn2022.github.io/).
- Dasgupta, Dipankar, Zahid Akhtar, and Sajib Sen. "Machine learning in cybersecurity: a comprehensive survey." The Journal of Defense Modeling and Simulation 19, no. 1 (2022): 57-106.
- Das, Lokesh Chandra, Dipankar Dasgupta, and Myounggyu Won. "LSTM-Based Adaptive Vehicle Position Control for Dynamic Wireless Charging." arXiv preprint arXiv:2205.10491 (2022), submitted to NeurIPS 2022.
- Kishor Datta Gupta and Dipankar Dasgupta. "Adversarial Attacks and Defenses for Deployed AI model." IT Professional Magazine, in press.
